About

Mission Statement

Our mission is to spread awareness of the main issues affecting bees and how people can help. Most people are aware that bees are in danger, but not of the specifics or what they can do about it. Our goal is to change that and help save the bees before it's too late.

About the Creator

My name is Anisha, and in 2020, when developing a project idea for a program called Girls With Impact, I decided to do a project related to bees. Through my research then, I learned more about how bees work and realized just how essential to the ecosystem they are. This led me to advocate for bee conservation and to continue to learn more. Also, I've never been stung by a bee before, and I decided that this would be a good way to repay them.
